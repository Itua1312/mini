Anchor Private School was founded in the year 2002 by Mr Itua.

Our Mission Statement

To produce students who can think for themselves, respect dignity of labour and appreciate good values of life. To inspire the children with a desire for achievement and self-improvement both at school and in later life. To impact both mental and physical skills and abilities necessary to live and contribute to the development of the environment and society at large.

TO ACHIEVE THESE:

The school curriculum is designed to instill in the children the quest for unrelenting pursuit of excellence. The broad curriculum stimulates and enhances the children thinking faculty. The school runs a diversified curriculum to cater for the differences in potential talents and abilities.
